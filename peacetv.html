Peace TV Bangla is an Islamic television channel that broadcasts religious and educational content in the Bengali language. As part of the Peace TV network, the channel is dedicated to spreading the teachings of Islam, promoting peace, and fostering understanding among viewers. Peace TV Bangla offers a variety of programs, including Quranic recitations, lectures by renowned Islamic scholars, talk shows, and educational series aimed at deepening the understanding of Islamic principles. The channel also addresses social issues from an Islamic perspective and provides guidance on living a life in accordance with Islamic values. Peace TV Bangla is watched by millions of Bengali-speaking Muslims around the world and is known for its commitment to promoting positive values and peaceful coexistence.
